Furthermore, an interaction network was established based on the two gut microbial groups identified at the genus level (Ruminococcus and Prevotella), 5 gut microbial groups identified at the species level (Prevotella copri, Ruminococcus bromii, Ruminococcus flavefaciens, Ruminococcus gnavus, and Ruminococcus champanellensis 18P13[T]), 14 metabolites (butyrate, alanine, leucine, isoleucine, glycine, proline, tartaric acid, glycocholic acid, fructose, propionate, glycerol, ursodeoxycholic acid, acetate, and succinate) associated with the gut microbiome in RA, and the one shared specific gene related to the gut microbiome (CXCL10).
